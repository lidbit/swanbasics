Quet moments?

Are quet moments possible with a 5-year old in tow?

Trying to code and play!

One can only imagine the unlimited amount of patience required to parent. Its not about you any more. It matters not what you planned. How much you need to get something done. It's about one more twirl.

"Please Mummy! Look at me! Look at my ballet turns!!!"

So on and on it goes. Endless unbound happy energy!

These are the happiest moments in life

These moments are so happy and so brief, that you do put your work aside. You do watch the ballet dances and the hilarious attempts at turns! The warmth and happiness will last a lifetime! Kids grow, memories stay! These special moments shine like jewels in the grey monotonous struggle that we call existence.
